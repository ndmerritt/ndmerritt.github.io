CollegeAdmit

The visual identity for CollegeAdmit was designed to reflect clarity and tradition. The logo features a clean, modern typeface that balances academic tradition with a contemporary tone. By utilizing a minimalist palette, the design avoids the heavy, cluttered look of traditional academic branding in favor of a bright, sophisticated aesthetic. This refined approach was intentional to reflect the unique service model that differentiates CollegeAdmit within the college placement landscape.
