Other Comments

I like the design of this site, it's simple and sleek looking, while also being very easy to read the information. My first suggestion would be to maybe add images to some of the pages to make it much more visually interesting and feel a bit more like a complete portfolio. My other suggestion would be to anchor the footer at the bottom of the page so that there isn't a gap between it and the bottom of the screen on the About, Project, and Contact pages. Overall, great job!
