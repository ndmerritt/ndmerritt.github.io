Cinnamon & Saffron Bakery

Cinnamon & Saffron began as a case study exploring how the warmth of a traditional European bakery could live authentically in a digital space. I wanted the branding to feel as intentional as the baking itself, so I crafted a logo that balances artisanal craftsmanship with a clean, timeless edge. To bring that experience to life online, I designed a fully responsive website centered on storytelling, ensuring the bakery’s unique charm translates beautifully to every screen.
