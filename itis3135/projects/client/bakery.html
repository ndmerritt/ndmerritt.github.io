Cinnamon & Saffron Bakery

Cinnamon & Saffron began as a case study exploring how the warmth of a traditional European bakery could live authentically in a digital space. I wanted the branding to feel as intentional as the baking itself, so I designed a logo that bridges the gap between old-world craftsmanship and contemporary minimalism. To complete the narrative, I developed an elegant menu and a storytelling framework that mirrors the artistry of the bakery’s signature breads and pastries.
